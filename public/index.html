SAMPLE
Marcus Cirino
PG · 6'1" · Class of 2027 · Overall: L7 All-Star
📊 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Marcus Cirino is a high-IQ floor general with elite visual processing and above-average reactive timing. His cognitive profile suggests a player who sees the play develop before it happens — the kind of point guard who makes everyone around him better. His primary cognitive identity is a court vision specialist with clutch-level reaction speed.

💪 COGNITIVE STRENGTHS

Court Vision (L9 Reflex — Hall of Fame): In the 99th percentile for visual processing. This player captures the entire floor in a split second. In transition, expect him to find cutters and trailing shooters that most guards miss entirely. He reads 5-out spacing before the defense rotates.

Reaction Speed (L8 React — MVP): Processes tempo changes at elite speed. Can operate effectively at 120 BPM game pace. This translates to steal anticipation, fast break decision-making, and the ability to adjust mid-play when the defense shifts.

⚡ AREAS FOR DEVELOPMENT

Play Recall (L6 Replay — Pro): While above average, complex 12+ step sequences show slight degradation under speed pressure. Recommend drilling full offensive sets at increasing tempo. Simon-says style drills with 8+ positions will strengthen this pathway.

🏀 ON-COURT TRANSLATION

Transition: Elite. Expect this player to push tempo and find the open man before the defense sets. Half-court: Strong. Reads pick-and-roll coverage effectively. Pressure: Above average reaction time suggests clutch performance under tight clock situations.

🎯 COACH RECOMMENDATION

Run this player as the primary ball-handler in transition. In half-court sets, position him as the decision-maker on pick-and-roll actions. Defensive assignment: put him on the opposing team's best playmaker — his reaction time and change detection will disrupt offensive flow.

📈 DEVELOPMENT PATH

To reach L8+ overall: Focus on THE REPLAY at Tempo and Elite speeds — drill complex sequences until they become automatic. Additionally, THE RITMO training will sharpen defensive rotation reads from L7 to L9 range.
