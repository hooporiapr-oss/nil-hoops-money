WHAT IS BPM BASKETBALL™?
The system behind the score
▼
BPM Basketball™ is a proprietary training system that measures how fast your brain processes the game.

BPM stands for Beats Per Minute — because every drill runs on rhythm — and Box Plus/Minus — the way real basketball measures impact.

3 Speeds: Training (60 BPM) · Tempo (90 BPM) · Elite (120 BPM)
10 Levels: L1 Rookie → L10 GOAT

Your BPM score is your basketball brain — measurable, trainable, and visible to every coach and scout who views your profile.
