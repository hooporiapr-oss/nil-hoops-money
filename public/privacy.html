PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. WHO WE ARE

Hooporia™ is operated by GoStar Digital LLC, a company based in San Juan, Puerto Rico. When we say "Hooporia," "we," "us," or "our," we mean GoStar Digital LLC.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you create a profile or use Hooporia, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address and password (managed securely through Supabase Auth).
  • Player profile data: first name, last name, profile URL (slug), position, height, weight, age, highlight video links, NIL bio, NIL contact email, and graduation year.
  • Media: avatar photos, cover photos, gallery images, and video uploads that you choose to add to your profile.
  • Cognitive performance data: scores, levels, speeds, and session data from our six cognitive drills (React, Recall, Reflex, Replay, Ritmo, Beat).
  • AI report data: when you generate a Cognitive Scouting Report, your drill scores are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to produce a personalized analysis.
  • Payment information: if you upgrade to Premium, payment is handled by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number — Stripe processes and secures all payment data.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, and general interaction patterns to improve the platform.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your player profile.
  • Display your public profile to coaches, scouts, programs, and other users on the Discover page.
  • Generate your AI Cognitive Scouting Report based on your drill performance.
  • Process payments and manage your subscription tier (free or premium).
  • Populate the Leaderboard with cognitive performance rankings.
  • Improve Hooporia's features, performance, and user experience.
  • Communicate important updates about your account or the platform.

4. PUBLIC PROFILE VISIBILITY

Your Hooporia player profile is public by design. This means your name, position, stats, cognitive scores, photos, videos, NIL bio, and NIL contact email are visible to anyone who visits your profile URL or browses the Discover page. This is intended to help you get discovered by coaches, scouts, and brands.

If you do not want certain information to be public, do not add it to your profile. You can edit or remove profile information at any time from your dashboard.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Hooporia uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database hosting, user authentication, and file storage. Your data is stored on Supabase's secure infrastructure.
  • Stripe — payment processing for Premium subscriptions. Stripe's privacy policy governs how your payment data is handled.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-powered analysis for the Cognitive Scouting Report. Your cognitive scores are sent to Anthropic's API to generate your report. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models.
  • Vercel — website hosting and serverless functions.
  • Google Fonts — web fonts loaded from Google's CDN.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Hooporia is designed for basketball players aged 13 and older. Users under 13 are not permitted to create an account. Users aged 13–17 must have a parent or guardian create the profile with them or provide consent, as acknowledged during signup.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has created a profile, please contact us.

7. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication through Supabase, and PCI-compliant payment processing through Stripe. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. DATA RETENTION

Your profile and data remain on Hooporia for as long as your account is active. The platform is designed so that your basketball story stays with you — from your first team to your last. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us and we will process your request.

9. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Object to certain uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

10. COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

Hooporia uses browser local storage to remember your language preference and authentication session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Hooporia after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
